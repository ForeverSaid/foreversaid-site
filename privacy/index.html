Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

ForeverSaid is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of the stories you entrust to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information: Your name, email address, and any other contact details you provide when purchasing our services or joining our waitlist.
  • Intake form responses: Information about the person being recorded, including their background, life details, and story preferences. This is used solely to create personalized interview questions.
  • Audio recordings: Raw audio files you upload for production. These contain personal stories and are treated with the highest level of confidentiality.
  • Payment information: Payment details are processed securely by our third-party payment processor (Stripe). We do not store your credit card information on our servers.
  • Website analytics: Basic, anonymized usage data such as page views and traffic sources to help us improve our website. We do not use this data to identify individual visitors.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver the services you purchased, including creating personalized questions and producing podcast episodes
  • Communicate with you about your order, delivery timeline, and any questions
  • Send you updates about ForeverSaid if you joined our waitlist or opted into communications (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve our services and website

3. How We Protect Your Information

Your family's stories are deeply personal and we treat them accordingly. Audio files are stored securely and access is limited to the team members directly involved in your project. We do not share, sell, publish, or distribute your audio recordings or personal content to any third party — ever.

Once your project is delivered and you confirm satisfaction, we retain your audio files for 90 days in case you need revisions or additional episodes. After 90 days, raw audio files are permanently deleted from our systems unless you request otherwise.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our business:

  • Stripe for payment processing
  • Podcast hosting provider for delivering your private podcast episodes
  • Email service provider for waitlist and customer communications

Each of these providers has their own privacy policies governing how they handle data. We only share the minimum information necessary for each service to function.

5. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality and anonymized analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not sell your data to advertisers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information and audio files at any time
  • Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@foreversaid.co.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. The stories recorded may include children's voices as part of family recordings, but all purchases and account management must be handled by adults.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to active customers and posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions or concerns about your privacy? Contact us at hello@foreversaid.co.
