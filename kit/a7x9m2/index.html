3

Record Your Sessions

This is the heart of the project. Take your time.

Work through the 5 episode themes at whatever pace feels right. Some families finish in a weekend. Others spread it across a month. The best conversations happen when nobody feels rushed.

💡 Quick tips

• Hit record before they know it — do small talk first, then ease into questions

• Keep water nearby — 30–60 minutes of talking is hard on the voice

• Don't worry about mistakes — the pauses and stumbles are part of who they are

• Back up your files immediately after each session

4

Edit & Produce

Light editing is all it takes. The authentic voice is the product.

Follow the editing tutorial to trim dead air, add intro/outro music, and export your finished episodes. We recommend GarageBand (Mac) or Audacity (free, any platform). Don't over-edit — your storyteller's real voice is what makes this special.
