Your payment has been received. Here's exactly what happens next, step by step.

1

Fill out the intake form — today

Click the button below to complete a short questionnaire (about 10 minutes) about the person being recorded. Tell us who they are, what stories matter most, and what you want preserved. This is what we use to create your personalized questions — the more detail you share, the better the questions will be.

2

Receive your question set — within 3–5 business days

We'll review your intake form, craft a personalized set of interview questions organized into 5 themed episodes, and email you a PDF with the questions plus a full recording guide with gear recommendations and tips for getting the best audio.

3

Record the conversations — at your own pace

Work through the questions at whatever pace feels right. Some families do it in a weekend. Others spread it across a few weeks. There's no rush — the best conversations happen when nobody feels pressured. Your package includes up to 4 hours of raw audio across the themed sessions. If you go over, no problem — additional time is available at $49 per additional 15 minutes.

4

Upload your audio — whenever you're ready

When your recordings are done, upload the audio files using the link we'll include in your question set email. We accept most common audio formats (MP3, M4A, WAV, etc.). No special software needed — just the files from your phone or recording device.

5

We produce your episodes — 10–14 business days

Our team listens to everything, organizes the content into themed episodes, adds intro/outro music, cleans up audio quality, generates transcripts, and creates your pull-quote cards. We keep the authentic voice intact — the pauses, the laughter, the way your storyteller finds their words. If there are any moments you'd prefer we remove, just let us know.

6

Receive your finished podcast — via email

You'll receive an email with your private podcast link, full transcripts for each episode, and your pull-quote cards. Share the podcast link with your family — it works in any podcast app (Apple, Spotify, etc.) or in a web browser. Listen anywhere, anytime, forever.
