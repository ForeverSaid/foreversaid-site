How it works

Simple enough to do yourself.
Meaningful enough to last forever.

You don't need to be a podcaster. The DIY Kit walks you through every step — from the first question to the finished episode. Think of it like a recipe: we provide every ingredient and every instruction. You just follow along.

1

Tell us about your storyteller

Answer a few questions about the person being recorded — their background, personality, and the stories that matter most to your family. Takes about 5 minutes.

2

Get your personalized question set

Our AI generates custom interview questions designed specifically for your storyteller — organized into 5 themed episodes. Not generic prompts. Deeply personal questions that draw out the stories only they can tell.

3

Record the conversations

Sit down with your storyteller and hit record. Use our recording guide for tips on gear, room setup, and how to put them at ease. Go at your own pace — some families finish in a weekend, others take a month.

4

Edit & produce your podcast

Follow our editing tutorial to add the included intro/outro music, trim any rough spots, and export your finished episodes. Light editing is all it takes — the authentic voice is the product.

5

Share with your family forever

Upload to a free podcast host, share the private link, and your family can listen from any device, anywhere, anytime. Create pull-quote cards from the best moments to frame or share.
